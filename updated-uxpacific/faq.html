Services

About Our Services

UX Pacific offers a comprehensive suite of design services including UI/UX Design, UX Audit, User Research, Product Design, Design Systems, and UX Consultation. Each service is tailored to meet your specific needs whether you're a startup, growing company, or enterprise.
A UX Audit is a comprehensive evaluation of your existing digital product to identify usability issues, friction points, and missed opportunities. If your product already exists but users are dropping off, struggling to complete tasks, or if your conversion rates are lower than expected — a UX Audit is exactly what you need. We deliver an actionable report with prioritized recommendations.

Process

How We Work

Our process follows a proven 5-step framework: Discover (user research, stakeholder interviews), Define (problem definition, opportunity mapping), Ideate (creative exploration, concept development), Prototype (wireframes to high-fidelity designs), and Validate (usability testing, iteration, delivery). This ensures every design decision is rooted in real user needs.
Project timelines vary based on scope. A UX Audit typically takes 1–2 weeks. A full UI/UX design project for a web or mobile app usually ranges from 4–12 weeks. Product design engagements can be ongoing. During our initial consultation, we'll give you a clear timeline tailored to your specific project.
Collaboration is at the heart of how we work. We schedule regular check-in calls, share progress through Figma or other agreed tools, and incorporate feedback in structured rounds. Our goal is never to deliver a surprise at the end — you'll be involved and informed throughout the entire process.
Our primary design tool is Figma for all UI/UX work — it enables real-time collaboration with your team. We also use tools like Maze for usability testing, Notion for documentation, Loom for async video walkthroughs, and Miro for workshops and brainstorming sessions.
